About Comunion

Comunion is a decentralized autonomous organization collaboration network, helps companies, organizations and individuals all around the world to create, manage and operate their own decentralized autonomous organization (The DAO).

Comunion aims to solve complex problems of org and social dilemma by establishing the decentralized collaborate org using swarm intelligence as the decision-making principle, to activate each individual’s creativity，reshape way of organizing to unleash productivity, make the world more efficient and fair. Do the labor value freer.

Product Features

Entropy Consensus

For the first-ever, we proposed the concept of entropy consensus, as the community consensus indicator - scientifically measured consensus of a collaborative organization system.

And this consensus will be used in the decision-making decentralized org to realize fair and efficient decision making.

Mulitichain Support

Comunion is open and extensible to run across multiple public blockchains, mainstream public chains such as ETH, EOS, etc.

Beside from Dapps development, Comunion supports any business model or scenarios that require effective communication and collaboration.

One Click

It’s easy to use by One Click, the OC concept is designed in every part of Comunion. Users can create and operate their own DAO very convenient by One Click.

ACCS

Anonymous Colloboration Credit System

Comunion defined the standard and formula of credit system in anonymous collaboration, called ACCS, this credit system will be a global standard credit system and connect to more dapps in the future.

DApp Store

Comunion supplies a full service from launch to promoting for Dapp developers, the smart contract based DApp store will make Dapps pricing and transactions be more transparent and effective, add-with the incentive Token system is to support developers to create premium dapps for the ecosystem.

Business Node

there are many nodes in comunion for all kinds of business, especially connect virtual economy to the real economy.

Applicable Cases

Business Company

To create a business entity on Comunion will be much easier, quicker and more efficient than the traditional way of registering in government departments，Base on the original IRO of creation meaning circulation, anyone can have their own business entity start from zero, and focused on the business itself with the help of Comunion’s ecosystem.

Social Organizations

Many NGOs is suffering from corruption, mismanagement of funds or financial inefficiencies that root from lack of openness and transparency, the Comunion's decentralized collaboration network can help NGOs collaborate resource openly and transparently to realize the good vision about human poverty elimination, education, environment, etc.

Freelancers

Comunion supplies task-delivery smart contract and third-party arbitration mechanism which can guarantee the interests of each participant, the freelancer is not only paid by the price of labor power. Instead, he or she uses the labor force to invest in global value targets, meanwhile capture the future capital profit.
